Flagship Project SaaS Platform

FlipWise Renovation & Flipping Management

"Manage your flips like a pro."

FlipWise is a full-stack SaaS platform built for real estate investors who are tired of spreadsheets. It centralizes every dollar, every hour, and every receipt across all active properties — from the first nail to closing day. I designed, developed, and launched this product end-to-end, including AI-powered receipt scanning, team collaboration tools, financial reporting, and a mobile-ready PWA experience.

Core Features

Multi-Property Portfolio

Track multiple flips simultaneously with real-time cost tracking across your entire pipeline at a glance.

AI Receipt Scanning

Upload any photo or PDF receipt. AI automatically extracts vendor, amount, and category — processed in under 2 minutes.

Team Collaboration

Invite workers via email. They log hours and submit expenses — you approve with one click. Full audit trail maintained.

Financial Reports

Breakdown by category, labor vs materials, estimated profit per property. Export to CSV anytime for tax prep.

Timesheet Approval

Workers log hours with category and hourly rate. You review and approve — every entry is tracked with a full audit trail.

Audit-Ready Records

Every action is logged. Deactivate workers without losing history. Your data is always intact and fully auditable.

How It Was Built

01

Problem Discovery

Identified a real pain point: real estate investors managing flips with messy spreadsheets and no centralized system for tracking expenses, labor, and receipts across multiple properties.

02

Design & Architecture

Designed the full UX flow from scratch — property onboarding, worker invitation, expense logging, and reporting. Prioritized simplicity: up and running in minutes, no setup headaches.

03

Development & AI Integration

Built the full-stack platform with React frontend, Node.js backend, and PostgreSQL database. Integrated AI receipt scanning via LLM API for automatic data extraction from photos and PDFs.

04

Launch & Iteration

Launched with a free tier and Pro plan ($97/mo). Implemented PWA support for mobile home screen installation. Continuously iterating based on user feedback from active investors.
