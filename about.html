About Me

From Courtrooms to Code

I'm Rodrigo Leite, a web developer who traded law books for programming languages. I bring the same analytical rigor and attention to detail from legal training into every line of code I write.

The Unconventional Path

My journey started in law school in Brazil, where I spent years studying criminal law, data protection, and legal frameworks. But somewhere between case studies and courtroom simulations, I discovered something unexpected: I loved solving problems through code more than through legal arguments.

In 2021, I made the leap. I moved to Michigan, enrolled in IT Management, and later pursued Applications Development at Lake Michigan College. What started as curiosity became an obsession — building, breaking, and rebuilding until things worked exactly as they should.

Today, I combine the structured thinking of a lawyer with the creative problem-solving of a developer. The result? Websites and applications that are not just functional, but thoughtfully designed, meticulously tested, and built to last.
